My objective as an engineer and a philosopher is to learn new things, structure that knowledge, and share it effectively with others--whether that is in the form of impactful features improving performance and experience, or as a seminar and paper. I am driven by complex questions with multiple stakeholders, and work to creative inclusive and practical solutions that push product forward.

I received a PhD in Philosophy from the New School for Social Research for my dissertation, Pregnancy Beyond the Natural. My research, as well as six years' experience teaching undergraduate philosophy courses, motivates my interest in tech's impact on health and the body.

Outside of work, I love sci-fi, ornate baking projects, and weight lifting.
