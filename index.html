myPillBox

HTML, CSS, React, JSX, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, Sequelize

myPillBox is a digital pill box and daily medication organizer app built with custom bycrypt auth. At a glance, myPillBox provides the time of day and intuitive navigation to a list of the pills you should be taking at which time of day, or a complete list of the doses assocated with a particular prescription.


KnitWhich

HTML, CSS, React, JSX

KnitWhich is a knitting utilities app, which accesses the Ravelry API. KnitWhich compliments Ravelry's incredible functionality and gives users a seamless on-the-go access to their personal Ravelry data. In addition, KnitWhich offers a Row Counters utility, where users can add and label row counters for different projects, eliminating the need for easily misplaced notions.

Streetstagram

HTML, CSS, React, JSX, Express, Sequelize

Streetstagram is a full stack app where users share streetscape photos. Streetstagram builds community by creating a platform for neighbors to share current events, artists to share their work, and for individuals to engage with each other by sharing images of their daily life. Streetstagram communicates with AWS cloud storage to host image urls.

Kitty Jungle

HTML, CSS, Vanilla JS

Kitty Jungle is a single-player game where players carefully befriend feral cats by petting them (clicking on them). The object is to pet the cat enough to make friends, but not so much as to overstimulate the cat.
